Scott-Lee Atchison is currently a Ph.D. student in Music Education at Pennsylvania State University. Before his enrollment at Penn State, Mr. Atchison served as the Director of Athletic Bands at the University of Kentucky and Associate Director of Bands at Texas A&M University -Commerce where he directed the athletic bands, conducted the symphonic band and concert band, and taught classes in music technology, music literature, and marching band techniques.
